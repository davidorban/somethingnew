Something New: AIs and Us

A book by David Orban about technology, artificial intelligence, and humanity's future

Technological Singularity is the hypothetical moment in time when self-modifying artificial intelligences arrive. Setting their own goals and methods to reach them, they'll change the face of the planet and the course of history.

This book exposes in an accessible way what the consequences could be of the exponential acceleration of technological change and how artificial intelligences could change many, perhaps all, the rules that guide our knowledge of the world.
